One Year in UAE

Dec 8th, 2012

I came to UAE in 8-12-2011 to visit some relatives and search for suitable job.

It was very good experience to come to Dubai and stay in the fastest growing country in the world, and to see life organization in this country.

After this year of life in Dubai there is a question many people ask me about:

Is UAE the best country to live in?

The answer to this question not easy, but from my point of view I can put several negative and positive points, i will start with the positive points:

  • Dubai is miniature model about business all over the world.

  • In Dubai you can see and work with people of all nationalities in the world.

  • In Dubai you can live a good life and all your needs and hopes are available.

  • In Dubai you can get good work experience (depends on your position and the company you are working in).

  • You can have good friends from other your country.

And other good points…

In the other side, i will reserve some of negative points and mention some:

  • In Dubai you live away from home, your family and old friends.

  • You cann’t do any plan for your future in this country because you will leave someday.

  • You may suffer from some of the political problems between your country UAE.

  • Its very hot in UAE, and the weather is very difficult.

And other points i cann’t say for now …
